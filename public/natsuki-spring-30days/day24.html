文法トレーニング

ノートに答えを書いてから、解答を確認しよう（全15問）

Q1 — 穴埋め

My father going to buy a new car.

is

My father is going to buy a new car. — 父は新しい車を買う予定です。

Q2 — 穴埋め

We going to have a picnic.

are

We are going to have a picnic. — 私たちはピクニックをする予定です。

Q3 — 穴埋め

I am not going give up.

to

I am not going to give up. — 私はあきらめない予定です。

Q4 — 和訳

The store is going to open at 10:00.

店は10時に開く予定です。

is going to open(開く予定) at 10:00(10時に)

Q5 — 和訳

Who is going to help you?

誰があなたを手伝う予定ですか？

Who is going to help(誰が手伝う予定)

Q6 — 英訳

彼女は大学で何を勉強する予定ですか？

What is she going to study in college?

What is she going to study(何を勉強する予定) in college(大学で)

Q7 — 英訳

私たちは一緒に働く予定です。

We are going to work together.

are going to work(働く予定) together(一緒に)

Q8 — 4択

The movie （　　） going to start soon.

ア. am　イ. is　ウ. are　エ. be

イ. is

主語がThe movieなのでis

Q9 — 4択

（　　） is going to cook dinner?

ア. What　イ. Who　ウ. Where　エ. When

イ. Who

Who = 誰が

Q10 — 4択

They are going to （　　） married.

ア. get　イ. gets　ウ. got　エ. getting

ア. get

going to + 動詞の原形

Q11 — 並べかえ

[ is / the train / to / arrive / going / soon / . ]

The train is going to arrive soon.

電車はもうすぐ到着する予定です。

Q12 — 並べかえ

[ who / help / going to / is / you / ? ]

Who is going to help you?

誰があなたを手伝う予定ですか？

Q13 — 書きかえ

She teaches English.（next yearを加えて未来形に）
→ She teach English next year.

is going to

She is going to teach English next year.

Q14 — 書きかえ

Tom is going to call.（Whoで疑問文に）
→ is to call?

Who / going

Who is going to call?

Q15 — 書きかえ

She is going to study math.（Whatで疑問文に）
→ is she to study?

What / going

What is she going to study?

  • What is she going to 〜?
