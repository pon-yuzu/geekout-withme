The Brunch Scene in Australia

Brunch has become more than just a meal in Australia — it is a lifestyle. Every weekend, cafés are filled with people who enjoy spending their mornings eating delicious food and drinking good coffee. The Australian brunch menu, which has been influenced by many different cultures, offers dishes like avocado toast, eggs Benedict, and açaí bowls. Avocado toast, which was made popular in Australia, has now spread around the world. Many cafés have created their own special brunch menus that change with the seasons. Brunch is often enjoyed with friends or family, and it is common for people to wait in long queues at popular spots. The atmosphere of a brunch café, which is usually relaxed and sunny, is something that Australians truly value.

3 Review
📍 The Grounds of Alexandria — Sydney
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The Grounds of Alexandria is the most amazing brunch spot that I have ever visited in Sydney. The café is set in an old industrial building which has been transformed into a beautiful garden space with plants and flowers. The food, which is prepared with fresh local ingredients, is absolutely delicious. I ordered the smashed avocado on sourdough bread, which was served with perfectly poached eggs and feta cheese. The portions are generous and everything is beautifully presented on the plate. The café also has a small farm where animals are kept, so children love visiting too. I have been here three times already, and each time I have discovered something new on the menu. This place is loved by both locals and tourists who visit Sydney.

5 🦘 Australia Tips

ブランチに誘うフレーズ

友達をブランチに誘うときは "Do you want to grab brunch this weekend?" や "Keen for brunch on Saturday?" が自然です。"Keen" はオーストラリアで「〜したい」という意味でよく使われます。

オーストラリアの待ち時間

人気のブランチスポットでは30分〜1時間待ちも珍しくありません。"How long is the wait?"（どのくらい待ちますか？）と聞いて、"Put my name down, please."（名前を書いてください）と伝えましょう。

6 Conversation
🎭 友達をブランチに誘って、メニューを決める
A (Koa):
Hey, are you keen for brunch this Saturday?
B:
Yeah, sounds great! Have you got a place in mind?
A (Koa):
There's a new café that has just opened near the beach.
B:
Oh nice! What kind of food is served there?
A (Koa):
They have all the classic brunch dishes — avo toast, pancakes, and eggs Benedict.
B:
I love eggs Benedict! Have you been there before?
A (Koa):
Not yet, but it's been recommended by many people who I trust.
B:
Should we book a table? Popular places are always packed on weekends.
A (Koa):
Good idea. I'll make a booking for ten o'clock. Is that too early?
B:
That's perfect! I can't wait. I haven't had a proper brunch in ages.
