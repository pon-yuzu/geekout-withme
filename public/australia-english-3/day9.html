Melbourne's Famous Coffee Culture

Melbourne has been recognised as one of the best coffee cities in the world. The city's laneways, which are narrow streets filled with cafes and street art, have attracted visitors from every corner of the globe. Melbourne's coffee culture was established by Italian and Greek immigrants who arrived after World War Two. A flat white, which was invented in Australia, has become the most popular coffee order in Melbourne. The baristas, who are highly skilled, have been trained to create beautiful latte art. Many cafes that are hidden in small laneways have been discovered by food bloggers and shared on social media. Melbourne has also been voted the world's most liveable city several times. The coffee industry, which employs thousands of people, has contributed significantly to the local economy.

3 Review
📍 Patricia Coffee Brewers
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Patricia Coffee Brewers, which is located in a tiny laneway near Flinders Street, has become my favourite cafe in Melbourne. The cafe, which has no seating inside, is designed as a standing-only space. This unique concept, which was inspired by Italian espresso bars, has been praised by coffee enthusiasts worldwide. I ordered a flat white that was made with single-origin beans from Ethiopia. The flavour was smooth and rich, and the latte art was beautifully crafted. The barista, who remembered my name from yesterday, recommended their cold brew, which has been specially prepared overnight. The atmosphere, which is created by the friendly staff and the aroma of freshly roasted coffee, has made this place incredibly popular among both locals and tourists.

5 🦘 Australia Tips

オーストラリアのコーヒー用語

オーストラリアでは 'Americano' とはあまり言いません。代わりに 'long black'（ロングブラック）と注文します。'Flat white' はエスプレッソにスチームミルクを注いだもので、カプチーノより泡が少ないのが特徴です。'Short black' はエスプレッソのことです。

メルボルンのストリートアート

メルボルンの裏通り（laneways）は合法的なストリートアートで有名です。特に 'Hosier Lane' は世界中のアーティストが壁画を描くことで知られています。アートは定期的に上書きされるので、訪れるたびに新しい作品が見られます。'street art' と 'graffiti'（落書き）は区別されています。

Alright, I'll give it a go then! And could I also get a pastry that's recommended?

Sounds lovely. I'll have that as well. How much is it altogether?

B:
I'll tap, thanks. By the way, could you tell me where Hosier Lane is? I've been wanting to see the street art.
