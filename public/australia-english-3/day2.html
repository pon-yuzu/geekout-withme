Upside Down Seasons

One thing that has surprised many Japanese visitors is the fact that seasons in Australia are opposite to those in Japan. When it is summer in Japan, it is winter in Australia. Christmas in Australia has always been celebrated during the summer, which means barbecues on the beach are a common tradition. The Australian summer lasts from December to February, and temperatures that are recorded in some areas can exceed 40 degrees Celsius. The northern part of Australia, which is closer to the equator, has a tropical climate with wet and dry seasons instead of the four seasons that are experienced in the south. Melbourne is known for its unpredictable weather, which has been described as having four seasons in one day. Understanding the climate is essential for anyone who is planning to live and work in Australia.

3 Review
📍 Great Barrier Reef
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

The Great Barrier Reef is the largest coral reef system in the world, which has been listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1981. It stretches over 2,300 kilometres along the coast of Queensland. I have visited this incredible place during the Australian winter in July, and the weather was absolutely perfect for snorkelling. The water temperature was comfortable, around 22 degrees Celsius, which is much warmer than you might expect during winter. The reef is home to thousands of species of fish, turtles, and dolphins that have been protected by strict environmental laws. Unfortunately, parts of the reef have been damaged by coral bleaching that is caused by rising ocean temperatures. Despite this, the areas I explored were vibrant and full of colourful marine life. I would strongly recommend visiting between June and October, which is considered the best season.

5 🦘 Australia Tips

オーストラリアの季節と服装

12〜2月（夏）は日差しが非常に強く、日焼け止めとサングラスは必須です。紫外線は日本の5〜7倍と言われています。6〜8月（冬）は地域によって差が大きく、シドニーでは最低10度程度ですが、北部のケアンズでは冬でも25度前後あります。

メルボルンの「1日4シーズン」

メルボルンの天気は本当に変わりやすく、朝は寒くても昼は暑くなり、午後に突然雨が降ることがあります。地元の人は「天気が気に入らなければ5分待て」と冗談を言います。重ね着（レイヤリング）が基本スタイルです。

季節を活かしたワーホリプラン

ファームジョブは季節によって収穫物が変わります。夏（12〜2月）はベリー類やストーンフルーツ、秋（3〜5月）はりんごやぶどうの収穫時期です。ワーホリの開始時期を季節に合わせて計画すると、仕事が見つかりやすくなります。
