Classic Australian Comfort Food

When Australians think of comfort food, meat pies and fish and chips are the first things that come to mind. The meat pie, which is considered Australia's national dish by many people, has been eaten here for over a hundred years. It is a pastry shell that is filled with minced beef and gravy. Meat pies are sold at bakeries, petrol stations, and football stadiums across the country. Fish and chips, which were originally brought from England, have also become a beloved Australian dish. Fresh fish, which is caught along the coast, is covered in batter and deep-fried until golden. The dish is usually served with thick-cut chips and a slice of lemon. These simple foods, which have been enjoyed for generations, hold a special place in the hearts of all Australians.

3 Review
📍 Harry's Café de Wheels — Sydney
⭐⭐⭐⭐

Harry's Café de Wheels is a legendary pie cart that has been serving meat pies in Sydney since 1938. This small shop, which is located near the harbour in Woolloomooloo, has been visited by tourists and locals for decades. The most famous item on the menu is the Tiger Pie, which is a meat pie that is topped with mushy peas, mashed potato, and gravy. I have tried meat pies at many places, but the flavour here is something that cannot be beaten. The pies are made fresh every day, and the pastry is always crispy and flaky. Photos of famous people who have visited are displayed on the walls. Although the shop is small, it has earned a reputation that is known across Australia. The only downside is that seating is limited, so most people eat standing up.

5 🦘 Australia Tips

ミートパイの食べ方

オーストラリアのミートパイは手で持って食べます。上のパイ生地をまず外し、中身にトマトソースをかけてから食べる人も多いです。"Do you want sauce with that?"（ソースいりますか？）と聞かれたら "Yes, please!" と答えましょう。

Fish & Chips のオーダー

"I'll have the fish and chips, please." で注文できます。魚の種類を選べる場合は "What fish do you have today?" と聞きましょう。Barramundi（バラマンディ）はオーストラリアの代表的な白身魚です。Chips は「ポテトチップス」ではなく「フライドポテト」です。
