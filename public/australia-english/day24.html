タックスファイルナンバーの取得

Tax File Number

A Tax File Number (TFN) is essential for working in Australia. Without a TFN, you will pay the highest tax rate of 47%! With a TFN, you pay much less tax. You can apply online for free through the ATO website. The application takes about 10 minutes. Your TFN will arrive by post in about 28 days. Give your TFN to your employer when you start work.
