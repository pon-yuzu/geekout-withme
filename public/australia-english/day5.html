危険な動物と対処法

Watch Out!

Australia has some dangerous animals. There are venomous snakes and spiders. The funnel-web spider is one of the most dangerous spiders in the world. In the ocean, you need to watch out for jellyfish and sharks. But don't worry too much! Attacks are very rare. Just be careful when you go hiking or swimming. Always check your shoes before putting them on!

5 🦘 Australia Tips

🕷️ よく見るクモ

Huntsman Spider（アシダカグモ）：大きくて怖いけど無害。家の中によく出る。
Redback Spider（セアカゴケグモ）：赤い模様が特徴。毒あり、触らない！

💡 覚えておこう！

靴を履く前に中を確認！
トイレの便座の裏も確認する習慣を。
ヘビを見たら静かに離れる。
海では地元のサインに従う。

6 Conversation
🎭 ハウスメイトにクモについて聞く場面
A (You):
Oh no! What is this spider?
B:
Let me see... It's a huntsman. Don't worry!
A (You):
Is it dangerous?
B:
No, it's harmless. But it looks scary, right?
A (You):
Yes, very scary! Can you get rid of it?
B:
Sure. I'll put it outside.
A (You):
Thank you so much! I hate spiders.
B:
You'll get used to it. This is Australia!
A (You):
I hope so! Are there any dangerous ones?
B:
Yes, watch out for redback spiders. They're small and black with red on their back.
