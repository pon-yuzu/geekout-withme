📍 My Goals for Australia
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

I have three main goals for my working holiday. First, I want to work in a restaurant and improve my cooking skills. Second, I want to save at least $10,000. Third, I want to visit the Great Barrier Reef and see koalas in the wild. I also want to make friends from different countries. I'm a bit nervous but mostly excited. I can't wait to start my adventure!

5 🦘 Australia Tips

📝 目標の立て方

具体的な目標を3つ決めよう！
例：「$〇〇貯金する」「〇〇を訪れる」「〇〇を達成する」
紙に書いて毎日見ると達成しやすい！

💡 出発前にやること

・パスポートの有効期限確認
・ビザの申請
・海外旅行保険の加入
・クレジットカードの準備
・初期費用の準備（$5,000〜）

6 Conversation

So, what do you want to do in Australia?

I want to work as a cook in Brisbane!
A (You):
That sounds great! How long will you stay?
B:
At least one year. Maybe two if I get a second visa.
A (You):
Are you nervous?
B:
A little bit. But I'm more excited than nervous!
A (You):
What are you looking forward to most?

Meeting new people and trying Australian food!
A (You):
I'm sure you'll have an amazing time!

Thanks! I'll send you lots of photos!
