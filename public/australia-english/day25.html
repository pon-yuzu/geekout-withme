Job Hunting in Australia

There are many ways to find a job in Australia. Job websites like Seek and Indeed are popular. Facebook groups for backpackers often post jobs. You can also walk into shops and ask if they are hiring. Having a good resume is important. In hospitality, experience and attitude matter more than perfect English. Networking and making connections can also help a lot!

📍 My Job Search Experience
⭐⭐⭐⭐

I spent two weeks looking for a job. I applied to many restaurants online but got no replies. Then I decided to walk around and ask in person. I printed my resume and visited cafés in the city. On my third day, a Japanese restaurant said they needed help! I had a trial shift and got the job. Asking in person really works!

5 🦘 Australia Tips

💼 仕事探しの方法

・Seek、Indeed、Jora（求人サイト）
・Facebookグループ（「Sydney Backpackers」など）
・Gumtree（クラシファイド広告）
・直接お店に履歴書を渡す（飲食店に効果的！）

💡 飲食店で働くコツ

・忙しい時間帯を避けて訪問（14時〜16時がベスト）
・笑顔で挨拶、やる気をアピール！
・RSA（お酒を出す資格）があると有利。
・「Trial Shift」は短時間の試用のこと。

6 Conversation
🎭 お店に履歴書を渡しに行く場面
A (You):
Hi! Are you hiring at the moment?
B:
Actually, we are! Do you have experience?
A (You):
Yes, I worked as a cook in Japan for three years.
B:
Great! Do you have your resume with you?
A (You):
Yes, here it is.
B:
Thanks. Can you work weekends?
A (You):
Yes, I'm available anytime.
B:
Perfect! Can you come for a trial shift tomorrow?
A (You):
Of course! What time?
B:
Come at 10am. See you then!
