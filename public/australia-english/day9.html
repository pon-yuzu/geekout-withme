カフェ文化の街メルボルン

Melbourne - The Coffee Capital

Melbourne is Australia's second largest city. It is famous for its coffee culture and street art. The laneways are full of cafés and galleries. Melbourne has four seasons, so the weather changes a lot. The city is known for being very multicultural. There are many festivals throughout the year. If you love food and art, Melbourne is perfect for you!

3 Review
📍 Degraves Street — Melbourne CBD
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

I found the best coffee in Degraves Street! The laneway is so pretty with all the cafés. I ordered a flat white and a banana bread. The barista was very friendly and asked where I was from. We had a nice chat about Japan. The atmosphere was so cool with street art everywhere. I want to come back every day!

5 🦘 Australia Tips

☕ メルボルンの基本情報

人口：約500万人
時差：日本+1〜2時間（サマータイムあり）
気候：四季あり、天気が変わりやすい
特徴：カフェ文化、アート、スポーツ

💡 メルボルンで注文するとき

「Regular」がSサイズ、「Large」がLサイズ。
「Takeaway」がお持ち帰り、「Have here」が店内。
サイズを聞かれたら "Regular, please" でOK！
