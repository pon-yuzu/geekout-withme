Brisbane - The River City

Brisbane is the capital of Queensland. It has warm weather all year round. The Brisbane River runs through the city centre. South Bank is a popular area with free pools and parklands. Brisbane is smaller than Sydney but growing fast. The cost of living is lower than Sydney and Melbourne. Many working holiday makers choose Brisbane for the relaxed lifestyle.

📍 South Bank — Brisbane
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

I love South Bank! I went to the free beach pool called Streets Beach. It's in the middle of the city! The water was clean and warm. I lay on the sand and relaxed. There are also markets on weekends. I bought a nice bracelet. The view of the city from South Bank is beautiful, especially at night.
